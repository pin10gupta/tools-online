How to Calculate EMI Easily

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the amount you pay every month to repay a loan. It includes both principal and interest components.

Why EMI Calculation is Important

Understanding EMI helps you plan your finances better. Whether you're taking a home loan, car loan, or personal loan, knowing your EMI ensures you don’t overburden your monthly budget.
