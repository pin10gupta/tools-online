What is BMI and How to Calculate

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple screening tool that uses your height and weight to estimate body fat. It helps categorize whether you are underweight, healthy, overweight, or obese.

What is BMI?

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Healthy weight: BMI 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 – 29.9
  • Obesity: BMI 30 or higher

How to Calculate BMI

Formula (Metric): BMI = Weight (kg) ÷ Height (m²)

Formula (US Units): BMI = (Weight (lbs) × 703) ÷ Height (in²)

Example: Weight = 70 kg, Height = 1.75 m → BMI = 22.9 (Healthy weight)

Limitations of BMI

  • Does not distinguish muscle from fat
  • Same chart for men and women
  • Not accurate for children, elderly, or pregnant women
  • Does not show fat distribution
