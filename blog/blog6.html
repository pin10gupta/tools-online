FAQs About Unit Conversion

Q: Why do countries use different units?
It’s based on historical practices. The metric system is widely used, while the US still uses imperial units.

Q: Is the metric system better?
Yes, because it’s standardized and easier to use, but imperial units remain common in some regions.

Q: Can unit conversion affect accuracy?
Yes, especially in science and engineering, where small errors can have big consequences.
