BMI Calculator

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. Use this tool to quickly calculate your BMI.

Understanding Your BMI

  • Below 18.5: Underweight
  • 18.5 – 24.9: Normal weight
  • 25 – 29.9: Overweight
  • 30 and above: Obese

Note: BMI is a general guideline and may not apply to athletes or people with high muscle mass.
